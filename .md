# **Mercedes SLK 55 AMG (2012) Performance Specs Documentation**
==========================

**Summary**
-----------

This document provides an in-depth look at the performance specifications of the 2012 Mercedes SLK 55 AMG. The documentation covers various aspects, including engine specs, transmission details, braking capabilities, suspension and handling, and more.

## **Engine Specifications**

The 2012 Mercedes SLK 55 AMG is powered by a 5.5-liter V8 engine that produces an impressive 415 horsepower at 6,800 rpm and 398 lb-ft of torque at 4,000 rpm. This potent powerplant features:

* Engine type: 90-degree V8
* Cylinders: 8 (2 banks of 4 cylinders each)
* Displacement: 5.5 liters (336 cubic inches)
* Horsepower: 415 @ 6,800 rpm
* Torque: 398 lb-ft @ 4,000 rpm

The engine is paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission that features a semi-automatic manual mode for added control.

## **Transmission Details**

The 2012 Mercedes SLK 55 AMG boasts a sophisticated 7-speed automatic transmission system that provides seamless shifting and excellent acceleration. Key features of the transmission include:

* Transmission type: 7-speed automatic with semi-automatic manual mode
* Gears: 7 (including reverse)
* Shift pattern: Semi-automatic manual mode for added control
* Torque converter lock-up: Yes, in certain driving modes

## **Braking Capabilities**

The Mercedes SLK 55 AMG features a comprehensive braking system that includes:

* Front brakes: Vented disc brakes with a diameter of 360 mm (14.17 inches)
* Rear brakes: Solid disc brakes with a diameter of 330 mm (12.99 inches)
* Brake type: Dual-piston front calipers and single-piston rear calipers
* ABS system: Yes, featuring Electronic Stability Program (ESP)

## **Suspension and Handling**

The SLK 55 AMG boasts an advanced suspension system that provides exceptional handling and stability on various road surfaces. Key features include:

* Suspension type: Double wishbone with coil springs and gas-filled shock absorbers
* Stabilizer bars: Front and rear stabilizer bars for enhanced roll control
* Steering: Hydraulic power steering with a rack-and-pinion setup

## **Dimensions and Weight**

The Mercedes SLK 55 AMG has the following dimensions:

* Wheelbase: 243.0 cm (95.67 inches)
* Length: 414.6 cm (163.23 inches)
* Width: 181.7 cm (71.54 inches)
* Height: 130.0 cm (51.18 inches)

The vehicle's curb weight is approximately 1,610 kg (3,548 lbs).

## **Performance Specs**

The SLK 55 AMG delivers impressive performance specs, including:

* Top speed: Electronically limited to 155 mph (250 km/h)
* Acceleration:
	+ 0-60 mph: 4.6 seconds
	+ 0-100 km/h: 4.5 seconds

## **Additional Features**

The Mercedes SLK 55 AMG offers various additional features, including:

* Sport-tuned suspension with adjustable damping
* Adaptive ESP system for enhanced stability and control
* Large 19-inch alloy wheels with high-performance tires
